I started my company, ArtListingPro, in 2023 and am working full-time on it. As a Solopreneur, I've learned to wear many hats. Coming from a background in Data Science and Machine Learning, the software side is my strongest suit.

ArtListingPro is a SaaS platform that lets you create high-ranking Digital Art Etsy Listings easily, allowing you to establish and scale a high-earning Etsy Shop within minutes.

For those less familiar: Crafting Digital Art has evolved with the help of AI Image Models, providing an opportunity to transform it into a side-hustle or even a full-time pursuit. This involves selling your creations on various platforms like Etsy, Ebay, Shopify, and beyond.

ArtListingPro takes care of the SEO and image processing tech stuff. It handles the mundane tasks so you can focus on the creative part. I think there are so many untouched ideas out there just waiting to be discovered.
